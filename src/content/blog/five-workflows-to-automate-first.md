Not every automation is worth building. Here are the five I recommend to every business owner I work with — ranked by ROI and implementation difficulty.

## 1. Missed call recovery

Every missed call is a lost customer. An AI voice agent that answers after hours and on weekends recovers revenue that would otherwise disappear. The economics are simple: if your average job ticket is 350 dollars and you convert 40 percent of answered calls, every 10 missed calls per week costs you 1,400 dollars per week.

## 2. Lead qualification

Manual lead qualification consumes sales time on leads that will never buy. An automated qualifier asks the right questions, scores the lead, and routes it to the right person or sequence. The time saved compounds.

## 3. Past client reactivation

Your best source of new revenue is your existing client list. A simple reactivation sequence that goes out every 90 days to dormant clients consistently generates bookings at near-zero cost.

## 4. Review request

Most businesses that deliver great service never ask for reviews. An automated request sent 48 hours after a completed job captures reviews at the moment satisfaction is highest.

## 5. Appointment reminder

No-shows cost money. A 24-hour and 2-hour reminder sequence cuts no-show rates by 60 to 80 percent depending on the industry.

These five workflows are table stakes. If you have not automated them, start here before building anything more sophisticated.
